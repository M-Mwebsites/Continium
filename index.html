CONTINIUM

About us

Continium Technologies is a fabless semiconductor company offering electronic design services for different kinds of Integrated Circuit products. Continium’s engineering team possesses 150 men years of electronic circuit experience with world-leading expertise in data converters, especially broadband continuous-time Sigma-Delta converters.

TECHNOLOGY

Our Solution

Our solution is an ASIC integrated wireless receiver with Continuous Time Sigma Delta Analog-to-Digital Converter which will enable base station manufacturers to develop future BTS systems supporting very high bandwidth at low power dissipation, at low cost and with a very small form factor. The advanced multi-channel single-chip receiver ASIC significantly reduces expensive and obsolete components used in the base station receivers nowadays and thus paves the way towards beam-forming active antenna arrays.
